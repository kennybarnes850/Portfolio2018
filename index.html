About Me

If you are looking for a passionate Web Developer/Software Engineer who is eager to learn and implement innovative technologies, Kenneth Barnes is the man for the job.Kenneth Barnes is a dedicated client services professional with demonstrated success in managing cases of individuals with a diverse set of needs. Throughout his career as a Serviceman in the Air Force and as a Full-Stack Development student, Kenneth has shown an eagerness to learn and grow within a progressive environment, affording him opportunities for professional development, team success and solid core values. During his enlistment, Kenneth oversaw the activities of other troops during successful stints in Operations Iraqi and Enduring Freedom. Kenneth successfully received his Bachelor's degree in Social Work, excelling inside and outside his classes through civic engagement and community activism. As a Social Services Case Manager who achieved a 96% success rate in the state of Georgia, Kenneth was able to communicate and execute client care plans with a multitude of diverse populations.

As a recent graduate of Georgia Tech’s Immersive Coding Bootcamp, Kenneth Barnes built Full-Stack applications that utilized the power of Javascript to make AJAX requests, manipulate JSON data and provide an overall positive user-experience with the use of CSS frameworks. His Friend Resource Sharing Application was built to provide peer-to-peer data sharing of websites links, images and other information. MySQL, Node.js and Express.js were utilized to capture, store and retrieve the data from an intuitive User Interface. Serving as the lead back-end developer for this group project, Kenneth quickly learned the necessary skills needed to complete the application while proving that he is a great team player and can be a leader in a team of developers. Kenneth’s passion for the process of building applications that can help others further encourages him to be a constant learner.
